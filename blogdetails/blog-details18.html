Algorithm Strategies

By Rahul V | Published: Oct 09, 2025

Algorithm strategies are essential for efficient problem-solving. Learn greedy algorithms, divide & conquer, and backtracking techniques. Greedy approach selects the best local option to achieve global optimum. Divide & conquer breaks problems into smaller, manageable sub-problems. Backtracking explores all possible solutions to find the correct one. Optimized algorithms improve performance and reduce computation time. Practicing these strategies is crucial for coding interviews and competitions.

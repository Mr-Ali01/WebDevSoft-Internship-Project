DSA Basics

By Rahul Verma | Published: Oct 04, 2025

Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) are fundamental for efficient problem-solving in programming. Arrays, linked lists, stacks, and queues provide the foundation for complex data handling. Understanding time and space complexity is essential for writing optimized code. Sorting and searching algorithms improve data retrieval performance. Recursive solutions often simplify complex problems and teach algorithmic thinking. Trees, graphs, and hash tables form the backbone of advanced algorithms. Practicing DSA problems regularly enhances logical thinking and prepares for coding interviews.

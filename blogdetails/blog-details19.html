Advanced DSA

By Priya S | Published: Oct 03, 2025

Advanced data structures are essential for complex problem-solving. Learn trees, graphs, heaps, and their algorithms. Trees provide hierarchical data storage for efficient searches. Graphs help model networks and relationships between nodes. Heaps are useful for priority queues and optimized sorting. Knowledge of advanced DSA is crucial for competitive programming. Implementing these structures improves coding skills and algorithmic thinking.

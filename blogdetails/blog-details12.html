Cybersecurity Tips

By Vikram R | Published: Oct 03, 2025

Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from attacks and unauthorized access. Using strong passwords and multi-factor authentication reduces security risks. Regular software updates patch vulnerabilities and prevent exploits. Anti-virus, firewall, and intrusion detection systems strengthen defenses. Awareness of phishing, social engineering, and malware is essential. Encrypting sensitive data ensures privacy and compliance. Adopting best practices helps organizations and individuals stay safe online.
